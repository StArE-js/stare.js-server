Alex Bregman, Houston-area mascots take 'The Stare' to another level of awesome

Amanda Cochran, Specialty Senior Content Editor

Published: December 10, 2018, 4:03 pm
Tags: Trending, Social, Weird News, Talkers, Sports

HOUSTON – Houston Astros third baseman Alex Bregman is known for his stare following big hits during the 2018 season. 

It's a move the team embraced after big moments and big wins. 

In a new video posted on the Houston Texans' mascot's Facebook page, Toro takes the move to a new level, showing Bregman and several Houston-area team mascots doing his signature move.
